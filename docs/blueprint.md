# **App Name**: Strikers Ledger

## Core Features:

- Admin Financial Dashboard: Secure dashboard for 'strikersudinur@gmail.com' to track total club revenue and deduct expenses for equipment or tournaments.
- Membership Fee Tracker: Interactive member list to mark the monthly ₹100 payment for Jathindas, Nived, and Arpit, which automatically updates the revenue.
- Personal Payment Portal: Members login using the club email and their phone number as a password to view their individual payment status.
- Automated AI Reminders: Generative AI tool that drafts and sends personalized notification alerts on the 5th and 21st of every month for outstanding dues.
- Treasurer Quick-Link: Direct communication module to contact the treasurer Jathindas (83300 39531) regarding any transaction discrepancies.
- Ledger Audit System: Maintain a timestamped record of all income (fees, tournaments) and expenses (buying equipment like balls).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated Trophy Gold (#B8860B) reflecting the sports heritage and competitive spirit.
- Background color: A very light, clean Cream (#FDFBF7) to keep the theme humanized, classic, and easy on the eyes.
- Accent color: Deep Laurel Green (#2E4733) used for status indicators and success states, symbolizing the lush Kerala landscapes.
- Font pairing: 'Playfair' for headlines to give a classic, elegant, and arts-focused feel, with 'PT Sans' for body text for modern readability.
- Hand-drawn style icons for sports (cricket bats, footballs) and finances to keep the UI looking humanized rather than technical.
- A spacious, editorial-style layout that focuses on white space and legible tables, moving away from cramped dashboard tropes.
- Soft fade-in transitions and subtle scale effects on button interactions to provide a premium, agency-quality user experience.
